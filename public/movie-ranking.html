The Best Movies According to PRASHANT

My top 3 movies of all-time.

The Shawshank Redemption

the crime elements are peripheral and there's more humor than one might find in a typical thriller.

Interstellar

The idea of time dilation due to gravity on the behemoth planet, wormholes, and even the black hole shown was ahead of its time.

The Matrix

innovative visual effects, action sequences, cinematography and entertainment value
